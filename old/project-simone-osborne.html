Simone Osborne

client

Simone Osborne

Aug, 2015 Launch
the brief

Simone Osborne, a leading soprano opera singer based in BC and Toronto approached me to create a fresh design for her promotional website. Simone had an online presence sometime before but hadn't seen much value from her current site. I was asked to design and develop a new look for Simones online presence.

what i did

Working closely with Simone I created a variety of initial mockups to discuss with the client. After initial designs and feedback was approved I was also responsible for implementing the design, optimizing assets and images, deploying the final site to the clients hosting provider and providing monthly maintenance

challanges i faced

Simone had a clear vision for her site from day 1, but she was struggling to develop a cohesive brand image and didn't have the time to invest in a full brand solution. By leveraging unique typography and Simones strong selection of photography we were able to create an elegant and harmonious brand.
