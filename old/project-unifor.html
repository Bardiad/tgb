Various Projects

client

Unifor

Sept, 2015
the brief

Unifor is one of Canada's biggest private and public sector unions. I had the opportunity to work with a variety of different departments at Unifor on a variety of print design projects

what i did

Unifor originally approached me looking for personnel training for Adobe InDesign and InCopy, showing them copywriters and designers can work collaboratively on a single document at the same time.

I really enjoyed working with the people I met and soon after was contacted for a variety of different projects including All In, a union recruitment campaign where I designed the complete 80 page booklet, the campaign logo, buttons and other marketing material.

I've also worked on various "get out the vote" print ads that appeared in the Globe and Toronto Star during the 2015 national election

I believed strongly in the core values that Unifor represents. THis was one of the reasons I enjoyed working with them so much. I firmly believe that design has the power to change our world and I think it is important to use that power to affect positive change
